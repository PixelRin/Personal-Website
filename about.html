About Me

Background

I am a 12th-grade student at UEIS with a strong interest in biology and the natural world. I am particularly drawn to marine ecosystems and hope to pursue a career in marine biology or biological research. My fascination with marine life, especially jellyfish, has shaped my academic interests and creative work.

Artistic Practice

Beyond science, I have a deep passion for artistic expression. I primarily use traditional and digital mediums, creating character designs and short form animatics. My creative work often draws inspiration from my favorite pieces of media and artists such as gachiakuta, omori, and Monet.
