Who I Am

Hello! I'm Egshiglen, a 12th grader at UEIS with a heart full of curiosity for the natural world. I'm passionate about becoming a marine biologist (or biologist in general!) because I believe every creature, from the tiniest plankton to the largest whale, has a story worth telling.

When I'm not studying biology or preparing for university, you'll find me with a sketchbook in hand, drawing animals, cartoon characters, or anything that catches my imagination. I love bringing characters to life through animation and art!

💙 "The ocean stirs the heart, inspires the imagination, and brings eternal joy to the soul." 💙
